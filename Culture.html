Omani Culture

Culture Highlights

  1. Traditions
    • Traditional Clothing: Dishdasha, Abaya, Kumma
    • Festivals : Muscat Festival, National Day
    • Handicrafts : Silver Jewelry, Pottery, Weaving
  2. Cuisine
    • Traditional Dishes: Shuwa, Majboos, Harees
    • Sweets: Halwa, Dates
    • Beverages: Kahwa (Omani Coffee), Laban (Yogurt Drink)
Clothing Item Description Cultural Significance Image
Dishdasha Long ankle length robe, usually white or light colored Everyday wear for Omani men, symbol of national identity and modesty
Kumma & Massar Traditional headwear for Omani men Symbol of cultural heritage and social status
Abaya & Hijab Traditional outer garment for Omani women Symbol of modesty and cultural identity
Khanjar Traditional dagger with a distinctive curved blade Symbol of Omani heritage and craftsmanship
