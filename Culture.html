Omani Culture

Culture Highlights

  1. Traditions
    • Traditional Clothing: Dishdasha, Abaya, Kumma
    • Festivals : Muscat Festival, National Day
    • Handicrafts : Silver Jewelry, Pottery, Weaving
  2. Cuisine
    • Traditional Dishes: Shuwa, Majboos, Harees
    • Sweets: Halwa, Dates
    • Beverages: Kahwa (Omani Coffee), Laban (Yogurt Drink)

Festivals

Oman hosts a variety of festivals throughout the year that celebrate its rich culture and heritage. The Muscat Festival, held annually in January, showcases traditional music, dance, crafts, and cuisine. National Day on November 18th is a significant celebration that includes parades, fireworks, and cultural performances. These festivals provide an opportunity for locals and visitors alike to experience the vibrant culture of Oman.
